What is RobotsConf?

RobotsConf is a revolutionary technology event for software developers to get an efficient and effective deep dive into the exciting world of hardware development. If you are a Ruby, Python, JS or any other high level language developer that is interested in the Maker Movement, but unsure where to start -- this event is designed for you. We will have experts in each high level langauge in attendance and available as well as many of the incredible makers who are already changing the world. With hands-on sessions covering drones, 3D Printing, microcontrollers, and the Internet of Things, this is the only two day event that will take you from a software developer to a Maker.

RobotsConf is organized by the same team as JSConf and done with the direct cooperation of the various hardware leaders of most high level langauges,
