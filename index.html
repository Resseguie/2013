What is RobotsConf?

RobotsConf is a revolutionary technology event for software developers to get an efficient and effective deep dive into the exciting world of hardware development on December 6th and 7th, 2013. If you are a Ruby, Python, JS or any other high level language developer that is interested in the Maker Movement, but unsure where to start -- this event is designed for you. We will have experts in each high level language in attendance and available as well as many of the incredible makers who are already changing the world. With hands-on sessions covering drones, 3D Printing, microcontrollers, and the Internet of Things, this is the only two day event that will take you from a software developer to a Maker.

Organized by the same team as JSConf, RobotsConf is purposefully being constructed to bring developers of all high level programming languages together for the love and education of hardware. The blossoming Maker movement and hackerspaces are amazing, but they can easily be a little scary or daunting to most software developers. This shouldn't be the case, which is why we created this event and we cannot wait for you to join us, in Florida, to build robots (and drones and other incredible things)! Register now for RobotsConf, overcome your fear of hardware, and become the next generation of maker.

How To Get Here!

Locate: Amelia Island

Nestled in Florida, just south of Georgia, the barrier island of Amelia Island is an absolute paradise, in part because it is remote location. The closest airport is Jacksonville International Airport (JAX), roughly 29 miles from the resort. If you opt to rent a car OR drive to Amelia Island, there is plenty of free parking on the resort If you do bring a car, you can head to the historic and beautiful Fernandina Beach.

See you on the island!
